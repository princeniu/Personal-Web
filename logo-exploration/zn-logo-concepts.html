My take

A is the safest winner for a recruiting portfolio: recognizable initials, crisp engineering feel, and compatible with the current SVG/hover system. B is the most conceptually yours if you want the mark to carry the UX/HFE “journey/path” idea. I would not ship D as-is; I would borrow its warmth only if A feels too cold.
